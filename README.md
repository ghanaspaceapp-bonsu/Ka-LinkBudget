This link budget tool was developed by Dr. Benjamin Bonsu, an RF expert with over 10 years of experience in lunar communications and space systems. Use this app to explore the LUNAnet V5 GMSK link chain, including Ground, Satellite, and Lander sessions.

Technical insight
This model emphasizes the physical requirements for a reliable lunar link: free-space path loss at Ka-band, realistic implementation losses, lunar noise temperature, and the relationship between C/N0, Eb/N0, and BER. A positive margin indicates the link meets the required Eb/N0, while the C/N0 analysis shows the operational receive sensitivity needed to support the target BER.
